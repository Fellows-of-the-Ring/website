Loopring

Decentralised Exchange
and Open Protocol

Loopring is an open protocol based on smart-contract for decentralized exchange on public blockchains. Loopring allows multiple exchanges to mix and match the same set of orders with off-chain order-matching and on-chain settlement. Our vison is to become the largest market marker in the growing multibillion landscape of cryptocurrencis, digital assets and blockchain transactions.

Features

Zero Risk

Loopring does not require customers to send tokens to exchanges for custody. Tokens always remain in customer's blockchain addresses during the whole lifetime of an order. Customers can even move their tokens around after orders are submitted - Loopring will automatically adjust trading amount at the initial price. Loopring protects customers from threats such as exchange bankrupcies and DDOS.

Ring-Matching

Loopring has redefined the meaning of order matching. In one trade, mutiple orders can be matched and settled in one transactional operation.

Order Sharing

Loopring orders can be broadcasted to multiple exchanges for competitive matching. One order can also be partially matched by more than one exchanges as well.

Decentralized

Orders are created, signed, and broadcasted off-chain; order clearence and settlement happends on-chain.

Such decentralized way of token trading guarantees zero-risk and high liquidity.

Cross-chain protocol

Loopring was designed to be blockchain agnostic. As long as a blockchain has smart-contract support, Loopring can be implementd, and all ERC20-like tokens on such a blockchain can be traded under Loopring.

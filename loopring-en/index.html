Loopring

Is A Decentralised Exchange
And Also A Protocol

Loopring is an opened protocol based on smart contract for decentralized exchange on the Ethereum blockchain. Loopring allows multiple exchanges to mix and match orders, off-chain order-matching and on-chain transaction clearing and payment. Loopring is on mission to become the largest market marker in the growing multibillion landscape of cryptocurrencis, digital assets and blockchain transactions.

New feature

Zero Risk

Loopring protocol does not require customers to send the token to a medium for custody order. Tokens still remain at the customers account when placement, settlement and order matching are processing. Traders can still trade or transfer their tokens in their account after placement made. Additionally, Loopring will automatically adjust trading amount at the initial price. This is why Loopring can protect customers from EXTERNAL crisis. Eg, exchanges close down, Dos attack.

Ring-Matching

Loopring has redefined the meaning of order matching. Where order matching system does not only matches buy and sell orders for token on one exchange. It can include multiple types of tokens with multiple exchange.

Order Sharing

Loopring’s order will be broadcast and matching traded to multiple exchanges under Loopring ecosystem. An order can be partially traded with exchange A and completed the rest trade with exchange B.

Price matching among the exchanges can efficiently and smoothly complete matching order. Loopring exchanges will not be affected by.

Decentralization

Matching Orders are made off-chain. Settlement and clearing are made on-chain. Technically, no risk exist. Asset is 100% safe in the account.

Cross-chain protocol

Loopring is a decentralised exchange and safe protocol, it's also executable for multi public blockchain. It will be started to implement on ERC20 first, following to EOS, ETC and Qtum in the future.
